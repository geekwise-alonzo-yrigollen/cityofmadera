Visitors

Just minutes from the Sierra Nevada Mountains and miles from the entrance to Yosemite, the city of Madera offers an oasis retreat in the heart of the San Joaquin Valley. Madera is less than two hours from three National Parks. A sampling of events in and around Madera that reflect the area’s colorful history and heritage include: Sierra Arts Trail, Coarsegold Peddlers Fair and Indian Fair Days.

As a premier residential community, Madera offers both residents and visitors alike an abundance of cultural events, special attractions, miles of trails and open spaces, challenging golf courses and outstanding recreational facilities as well as renowned resorts, restaurants and shopping.

  • Take part in the Madera Wine Trail Weekends or explore the Madera Wine Trail
  • Visit some of the areas many museums and cultural centers
  • Enjoy a look at years gone by and take part in Madera Old Timers Celebration
  • Or keep your eye on the sky at the Legends over Madera Airshow

From luxurious hotels, great dining, championship golf courses, family-family amenities, and spectacular events, Madera draws visitors from around the world. Visit Madera to explore all of the exciting possibilities available for you in Madera…a place to play!
