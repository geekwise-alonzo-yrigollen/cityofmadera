MADERA CITY COUNCIL The City of Madera was incorporated on March 27, 1907. It is a general law city which operates under the Council/Manager form of government. The Madera City Council, a board of seven, is the elected legislative body of the City of Madera. Mbbers of the City Council are elected by district and the Mayor is elected at large.
