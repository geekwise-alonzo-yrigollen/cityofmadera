The Municipal Code is updated, at minimum, sbi-annually. To access the Madera Municipal Code, click here. For a listing of ordinances adopted after 3/19/08, click here. Copies containing the full text of listed ordinances may be viewed or purchased for a fee at the Office of the City Clerk, 205 West Fourth Street, Madera, California, 93637.
