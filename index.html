The ARRA awards $980 million in Community Development Block Grant-Recovery (CDBG-R) funds through the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD). To distribute these funds to cities, counties, insular areas and states, HUD has suggested a format for grantees to follow. Our CDBG-R grant amount is $260,115.
